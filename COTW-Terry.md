# Terry Bogard - Quick Guide
![Description](https://static0.thegamerimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/wm/2025/04/fatal-fury-city-of-the-wolves-terry-guide-featured-image.jpg)

<u>Controls:</u> A=LP - C=HP - B=LK - D=HK  

## Neutral / Strategy
#### Zoning (high-range)
* `236A` ==> walk / anti-air
* `236PP` ==> run pressure / anti-air
* `214D` ==> neutral skip
#### Pokes (mid-range)
* `5C` / `2C` (hit-confirm)
* `5D` (only super-cancelable)
#### Pressure (close-range)
* `5C.6C` (hit-confirm)
* `5C.6C` > `feint` > `5C` (frametrap or hit-confirm)

## Hit-confirms
* `2B` > `2A` > `236B.6K.6K`
* `2B` > `5A` > `623D Break` > `214P/PP` ...
* `5C.6C` > `214P/PP` ...
* `5C` / `2C` > `214P/PP` ...
* `5C.6C` > `feint` > `2C` > `214P/PP` ...

## REV ARTS (EX) routes 
> after 623D Break / 5C.6C / 5C / 2C
*  `214AC,214BD` > `623D Break` > `236B.6K.6K`
*  `214AC,214BD` > `623D Break` > `623KK` > `Super.A/C`  
*  `214AC,236KK.6K.6K` > `623KK` > `Super.A/C` 
*  `236KK.6K.6K,214AC,214BD` > `623D Break` ...

## Punish (counter-hit)
* `5D` > `5C` …
* `214A` > `214KK` > `623D Break` …

## Frametraps
* `236KK` > `5C`
* `5C.6C` > `Feint` > `5C`
* `5D` > `5A`
* `5B` > `5A`

## Meaty
> after Throw / Super / REV Blow
* `Throw` > `214A Break` > `5B/5C`
* `Super.A/B` > `214C` > `2C`
* `REV Blow` > `Jump` > `5C`

## Safe jump
* `Super.A` > `214A Break` > `jC`
